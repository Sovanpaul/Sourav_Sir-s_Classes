NOTES AND STUDY MATERIALS

Our faculty of highly qualified teachers and researchers have combined through several books and journals, having collected and processed all the information so that it becomes easily accessible to the student. The study material is prepared in a time tested fashion with the student kept in mind in such a fashion that the student can catch up on the various topics in the event of missed lectures. The study notes are designed in a fashion so as to complement the material discussed in our tuition classes. We have also received an overwhelming response from the entire student community that, even if it is not possible for students to attend tuition regularly, they can follow up easily. SCHOLARSHIPS TO BPL STUDENTS

Here at Sourav Sir's Classes, there is a continued effort to help students excel and pursue their aim without any hindrance. In a bid to do that, our institute has begun a unique initiative to help meritorious students belonging to families from the Below Poverty Line (BPL). Selected students will receive special scholarships and would not be required to give any course fee while preparing for the prestigious entrance exams from our institute.
